Prev step   Step 5   

You are almost ready to put gWLM into action.

In this step:

  1. Review each SRD.

    Tables at the bottom of the window show the workloads for each SRD you are about to create.

    To change the mode, compartment type, or associated policies, select the [Prev] button until you reach the appropriate window, make your edits, then repeatedly select the [Next] button to return to this point.

  2. Select the [Finish] button to begin managing all the listed SRDs.
